Healthy Zucchini Fritters

Mix the grated zucchini with the salt and let it sit for 5 minutes to remove any moisture. Place it in a sieve (strainer) set over the sink and press down on the zucchini to remove as much liquid as possible.

Pour the squeezed zucchini into a large bowl and add the cottage cheese, chickpea flour, egg, baking powder, granulated garlic and ground cumin. Stir it all together until completely mixed.

Heat half the oil in a medium nonstick skillet over medium-low heat. Drop 3 tablespoons of batter into the pan and flatten slightly. Let it cook until the bottom is browned, then flip it over and brown the other side.

In a small bowl, mix the mint and salt with the yogurt, then dredge in the harissa. Serve with the fritters to dip them.
