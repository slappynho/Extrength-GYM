Cake of oatmeal with chocolate

Preheat the oven to 180C with a fan.

In a medium bowl, mix the oats, banana whey protein, cocoa powder, and baking powder. Add the egg white, the mashed banana and the water, and stir until you obtain a homogeneous mass.

Pour the mixture into a small baking dish, making sure there is room for it to rise, and bake for 12-15 minutes, until the oats are cooked through.

Remove the rolled oats from the oven and, while still warm, drizzle with the protein cream, sprinkle with the chocolate chunks, and serve. Optionally, you can cover the rolled oats with a few slices of banana and Greek yogurt.
